⚙️ Engineering Careers

Engineering offers some of the highest-paying and in-demand careers in India and globally. With branches spanning computer science, electronics, mechanical, civil and more, there's a path for every aptitude. Top entrance exams: JEE Main, JEE Advanced, EAMCET.

Computer Science (CSE)

Software development, AI, machine learning, web and app development, cloud computing.

Jobs: Software Engineer, Data Scientist, DevOps, AI Engineer
₹4L – ₹40L+ per year

Electronics & Communication (ECE)

Embedded systems, VLSI, telecommunications, IoT, signal processing.

Jobs: VLSI Engineer, Network Engineer, Embedded Developer
₹3.5L – ₹25L per year

Mechanical Engineering

Design, manufacturing, thermal systems, robotics, automobile engineering.

Jobs: Design Engineer, Production Manager, Automobile Engineer
₹3L – ₹20L per year

Civil Engineering

Construction, structural design, transportation, urban planning, project management.

Jobs: Site Engineer, Structural Engineer, Urban Planner
₹3L – ₹18L per year

Electrical Engineering (EEE)

Power systems, renewable energy, control systems, electrical machines.

Jobs: Power Engineer, Control Systems Engineer, Energy Analyst
₹3.5L – ₹20L per year

Information Technology (IT)

Software systems, networks, cybersecurity, database management, cloud computing.

Jobs: System Analyst, Cybersecurity Expert, Network Admin
₹4L – ₹35L per year

🏥 Medical & Health Sciences

Medicine and healthcare offer noble and well-paying careers with strong job security. From MBBS to Pharmacy, Nursing to Dental — the field is vast. Top entrance exam: NEET UG.

MBBS

General medicine, specializations in surgery, pediatrics, cardiology, and more.

Jobs: Doctor, Surgeon, Specialist, Researcher
₹8L – ₹60L+ per year

BDS (Dental)

Dental surgery, orthodontics, endodontics, oral pathology.

Jobs: Dentist, Orthodontist, Oral Surgeon
₹5L – ₹30L per year

B.Pharmacy

Drug formulation, pharmacology, clinical research, regulatory affairs.

Jobs: Pharmacist, Clinical Researcher, Drug Inspector
₹3L – ₹15L per year

B.Sc Nursing

Patient care, ICU, operation theater, community nursing, midwifery.

Jobs: Staff Nurse, ICU Nurse, Community Health Worker
₹2.5L – ₹12L per year

🔬 B.Sc Sciences

Science degrees open doors to research, teaching, government labs, and postgraduate studies. With M.Sc and PhD, career prospects improve significantly.

B.Sc Physics

Research, aerospace, defense, data analysis, academics.

Jobs: Physicist, DRDO Scientist, Data Analyst, Professor
₹3L – ₹18L per year

B.Sc Chemistry

Chemical industry, pharmaceuticals, forensics, quality control.

Jobs: Chemist, Lab Analyst, QC Officer, Research Scientist
₹2.5L – ₹15L per year

B.Sc Mathematics

Data science, actuarial science, finance, academics, software.

Jobs: Data Analyst, Actuary, Financial Analyst, Teacher
₹3L – ₹20L per year

B.Sc Computer Science

Programming, software development, IT, machine learning.

Jobs: Software Developer, Data Scientist, Web Developer
₹3.5L – ₹25L per year

💼 Commerce & B.Com

Commerce opens up careers in banking, finance, accounting, and management. CA, CMA, and MBA are popular post-graduate options that greatly enhance earning potential.

B.Com (General)

Accounting, taxation, auditing, banking, financial services.

Jobs: Accountant, Tax Consultant, Bank Officer
₹2.5L – ₹12L per year

B.Com (Computers)

Business software, ERP systems, e-commerce, digital finance.

Jobs: Business Analyst, ERP Consultant, Fintech Analyst
₹3L – ₹16L per year

CA / Chartered Accountancy

Audit, taxation, financial planning, corporate finance.

Jobs: Chartered Accountant, CFO, Finance Manager
₹7L – ₹50L+ per year

BBA / MBA

Business management, marketing, operations, HR, entrepreneurship.

Jobs: Manager, Marketing Head, HR Officer, Entrepreneur
₹4L – ₹40L per year

🎨 Arts & Humanities

Arts and humanities offer diverse careers in civil services, law, journalism, education, and creative fields. UPSC, civil services, and teaching are popular choices.

B.A Political Science

Civil services, politics, law, journalism, public administration.

Jobs: IAS/IPS Officer, Journalist, Political Analyst
₹5L – ₹30L per year

B.A Economics

Finance, research, policy, banking, data analysis.

Jobs: Economist, Policy Analyst, Finance Officer
₹3L – ₹20L per year

B.A English

Teaching, content writing, journalism, translation, media.

Jobs: Content Writer, Teacher, Journalist, Editor
₹2.5L – ₹12L per year

B.A Psychology

Counseling, clinical psychology, HR, social work, research.

Jobs: Counselor, Psychologist, HR Specialist
₹3L – ₹18L per year

🔧 Diploma / Polytechnic

Diploma courses are 3-year programs after Class 10 or 12 that give practical, job-ready technical skills. Diploma holders can also join B.Tech in the 2nd year via lateral entry.

Diploma in Mechanical Engg.

Machine maintenance, production, automobile, fabrication.

Jobs: Maintenance Technician, Production Supervisor
₹2L – ₹8L per year

Diploma in Civil Engg.

Site supervision, surveying, construction, AutoCAD drafting.

Jobs: Site Supervisor, Draftsman, Quality Inspector
₹2L – ₹8L per year

Diploma in ECE

Electronics repair, telecommunication, networking, PCB design.

Jobs: Electronics Technician, Network Technician
₹2L – ₹9L per year

Diploma in Computer Science

Programming, web development, data entry, networking.

Jobs: Junior Developer, Data Entry, IT Support
₹2.5L – ₹10L per year

Entrance Exam Information

Eligibility, dates, syllabus, and tips for all major entrance exams

Engineering Exams
For B.Tech / B.E admissions across India
JEE Main
Joint Entrance Examination – Main
Eligibility:Class 12 with PCM, min 75% Mode:Computer Based Test (CBT) Sessions:January & April every year Subjects:Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics Duration:3 hours For:NITs, IIITs, CFTIs, state colleges
🏛️ NITs / IIITs
JEE Advanced
Joint Entrance Examination – Advanced
Eligibility:Top 2.5 lakh JEE Main qualifiers Mode:Computer Based Test (CBT) When:May/June every year Subjects:Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics Duration:3+3 hours (2 papers) For:IITs — 23 IITs across India
🏆 IITs Only
TS EAMCET
Telangana State EAMCET
Eligibility:Class 12 PCM / PCB, min 45% Mode:Computer Based Test When:May every year Subjects:Maths, Physics, Chemistry (or Biology) Duration:3 hours For:TS Engineering & Medical colleges
📍 Telangana
AP EAMCET
Andhra Pradesh EAMCET
Eligibility:Class 12 PCM / PCB, min 45% Mode:Computer Based Test When:May every year Subjects:Maths, Physics, Chemistry (or Biology) Duration:3 hours For:AP Engineering & Medical colleges
📍 Andhra Pradesh
Medical Exams
For MBBS / BDS / Allied Health Sciences
NEET UG
National Eligibility cum Entrance Test – UG
Eligibility:Class 12 PCB, min 50% (40% for reserved) Mode:Pen and Paper (OMR Sheet) When:May every year Subjects:Physics, Chemistry, Biology Duration:3 hours 20 minutes For:MBBS, BDS, AYUSH, Nursing, Pharmacy
🏥 Medical – MBBS / BDS
Postgraduate / Other Exams
For M.Tech, MBA, and other PG admissions
GATE
Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering
Eligibility:B.Tech / B.E graduates or final year Mode:Computer Based Test When:February every year For:M.Tech at IITs/NITs, PSU jobs
🎓 M.Tech / PSU Jobs
BITSAT
BITS Admission Test
Eligibility:Class 12 PCM, min 75% Mode:Computer Based When:May–June every year For:BITS Pilani, Goa, Hyderabad campuses
🏛️ BITS Pilani / Goa / Hyd
